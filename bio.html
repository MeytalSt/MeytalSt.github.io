About Me...

URBAN UNICORN

Most people ask me how to pronounce my name - may-TAHL. My name means water of dew in Hebrew. I was born in Jerusalem, Israel and moved to Minnesota in 2011 with my husband and 2 daughters.

In college I studied early childhood education. I never became a teacher, however I use the skills I learned in my personal life. After college I started working for my family’s humanitarian aid organization, where I was an administrative assistant and department manager.

Fast forward a few years and I was ready for a change. I went to work for a Christian TV station in Jerusalem as a network planner. After my girls were born, I took a step back from working full time and focused on raising them.

I went back to working for my family’s organization after moving to Minnesota. Throught my role as a global content manager, my eyes were opened to the world of UX/UI design. I was itching to do something creative and further my education, and so decided to study UX/UI design.

BEYOND DESIGN

When I’m not designing websites and apps, you can find me working out at the local gym, hanging out with my family and friends, swimming at the lake or reading a good book.

I love to dance, cook and dream of making pottery one day.
